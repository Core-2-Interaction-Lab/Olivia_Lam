Find out which borough has the best street pavement ratings for nice streets to walk on. This data is provided by the New York City Department of Transportation (NYC DOT) and they are responsible for keeping the city's streets in good repair. 💪🏻 🛣 🚧 🚏 🚦

In general, NYC DOT have inspectors rate New York City street conditions once every two years. The ratings are entered digitally into a Street Ratings applications system.
